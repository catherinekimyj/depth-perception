Welcome to Depth Perception

Depth Perception is a meditation visualization app that allows you to write down the thoughts that manifest your mind and visualize letting go.

There is a space for your name and a place for you to write down your train of thoughts.
Neither fields are required.

If there isn't much to say (leave the thought field blank), you will see that there is a randomly selected message of mindfulness and meditation from us upon submitting the form (like a fontune cookie!). Choose a sound of your liking and watch your thoughts dissipate over the duration of the music. The speed of dissipation will depend on the length of your thought and the length of the chosen sound.
